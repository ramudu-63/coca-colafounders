Javier Gueudet

“Do what you love.”

Javier is an Industrial Engineer and a passionate entrepreneur who strongly believes that startups are necessary for the developing of any economy, especially in developing countries.

Since he was in college he has worked with entrepreneurs from diverse industries including agriculture, biotech, food, media, and fashion - and other times as a team member and others as a mentor. He also worked for a non-profit organization that offered many solutions for entrepreneurs such as training, acces to risk investment, mentoring and networking. In this role he designed, launched and managed an accelerator program in partnership with Buenos Aires City Government. Javier is an active business angel investor and board member. 

He loves sports including rugby, tennis, football, and golf. He has two sons - Juan and Valentín, and wife Angui.
