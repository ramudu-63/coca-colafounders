Stefan Jacob

“Figure out where you want to go in life, make decisions based on how you want to get there and don’t worry if you end up somewhere different.”

Stefan has a blended background as a strategy consultant, energy trader and social entrepreneur. Having worked on projects in Mexico, Ethiopia and throughout Asia, he is as comfortable in a boardroom as a village hut. Originally from Boston, he came to Singapore in 2010 to start a new social enterprise and then used his startup experience to build the Urban Prototyping (UP) platform with Newton Circus and support the crazy innovations it created.

Outside of work, Stefan can be found hosting dinners, BBQs and wine tastings – really, anything involving friends, food and drink – while burning off those calories through yoga, running and dancing.
