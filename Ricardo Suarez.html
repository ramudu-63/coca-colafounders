Ricardo Suarez

“When you are experiencing a difficult moment, when everything looks dark…look back and see all the successes that you have had.”

Ricardo has been founding startups since he was 20, and has international experience and success. Ricardo founded Yumbling in 2011, a SOLOMO (Social-Local-Mobile) platform focused on leisure information that was acquired by Grupo Expansion (Time Inc.) in 2013. He led the growth and expansion of software startup JackBe in Mexico and Latin America, which was acquired by Software AG in 2013. Ricardo enjoys advising early stage startups and collaborating with other local entrepreneurs. He has been recognized by El Universal as one of the top seven innovative entrepreneurs in Mexico. 

He is a soccer fan, and still enjoys playing it. While not working on a startup, he really loves to spend time with his wife and two daughters.
