Idan Dadon

“The meaning of life is to find your gift. The purpose of life is to give it away.”

Idan is an efficiency-oriented innovator with an Honors MBA @(BGU & Columbia) and BSc in Information Systems. He has entrepreneurial experience in both high and low tech sectors (MultiDine.com, GemsNWine.com, TheTime4Wine), has founded and managed various successful businesses, and has issued various patents. With a strong technical background, Idan is able to implement creative solutions to finished products while obtaining a business-driven and effective approach. He is hands on with a diversified skill-set obtained from different industries, has good strategic appreciation and vision, and is able to build and implement sophisticated plans. He is self-driven and self-reliant; sets aim at targets and leads by example; takes a collaborative approach; and has good interpersonal skills to engage, motivate and encourage others. 

A nature lover and a farmer, when away from tech, Idan can be found in his vineyard, with his vines and wine.
