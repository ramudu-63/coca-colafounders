Gian Martinez

“Let’s create the world we want to live in.”

Gian was an intrapreneur at The Coca-Cola Company. As Head of Creative Excellence in Brazil from 2007-2013 he co-authored the global vision for the future of Content (Liquid & Linked), co-founded Coletivo Coca-Cola (shared value company) and led several global and Latin American marketing programs, changing the advertising, entertainment and licensing culture at Coca-Cola while achieving record business results. His passion to create big businesses by solving big problems moved him to become a formal entrepreneur.

Originally from Colombia, he lived in different countries during his childhood until moving to Rio de Janeiro at the age of 17. Gian is a creative leader who is passionate about people leadership, storytelling and furniture design.
