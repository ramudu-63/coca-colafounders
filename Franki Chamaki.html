Franki Chamaki

“Fall in love with the problem, not the solution.”

Franki started his career in finance and has held roles across Marketing, eBusiness, Project Management, Design and Design Thinking. Before joining the Founders platform, Franki worked in strategy and innovation for AMP. Here Franki built two direct digital business lines, co-designed the Design Thinking process and established the company’s entire innovation crowdsourcing program where he achieved a 17% (on average) idea to implementation conversion. 

Franki specializes in areas of Open Innovation, Crowdsourcing, Lean Startup and Design Thinking. He has founded several startups including iRomantic (shakeyourlove.com), mockupmytattoo.com, spaceble.com, and most recently TinderCompanion (tindercompanion.com). Franki also mentors various Australian startups and is heavily connected with the Australian startup community.
