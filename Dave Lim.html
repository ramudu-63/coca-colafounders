Dave Lim

“If you think you're a leader and no one is following you, you're only taking a walk.”

Dave considers himself to be a polymath, provocateur, pioneer and possibilist. Since 1996 he has worked on many sides of the entrepreneurial fence, as a venture capitalist and entrepreneur in the Pacific Rim and Silicon Valley, innovation facilitator, angel investor, marketer, educator, central banker and global fund manager.

Dave was born in Singapore and has lived in Canada, Australia and the US. He has studied at Stanford, Berkeley, University of New South Wales, and University of British Columbia. He loves to cook, travel to discover, has driven across two continents and once circumnavigated the world for six months by backpack.
