AJ Brustein

“Give me liberty, or give me death.”

Prior to joining the the Founders platform, AJ was a Senior Global Brand Manager for The Coca-Cola Company and has 10 years of brand marketing experience. He managed execution for some of the world’s biggest and most iconic brands and was heavily focused on brand strategy and global campaigns, with a focus on digital communication and pack / equipment / product / communication innovation. He led numerous award-winning projects including Where Will Happiness Strike Next which became one of the most awarded Coca-Cola programs of the past decade and continues to gather accolades, PlantBottle packaging, and Furu Furu Shaker, which was named the top product launch in Japan in 2009. He has been building brands both from a global role and has years of in-market experience in Japan, always focused on delivering brand love and brand value in an ever-evolving global marketplace.
