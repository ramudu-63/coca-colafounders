Rolando Meyer

“Never go it alone.”

Before joining the Coca-Cola Co-Founder Network, Rolando was mentoring entrepreneurs and consulting major corporations with business design projects. He is a Design Thinking Professor at the Universidad de San Andrés and has been invited to give Design Thinking and Entrepreneurship talks at diverse events. Rolando is a long-time entrepreneur - he co-founded a vertical garden company called Elevate and designed a toy brand called Waapos. He is a Strategic Design Consultant for the IAE Naves business plan competition. 

He has an Industrial Engineering degree from ITBA in Buenos Aires and Master of Arts in Business Design from Domus Academy in Milan. He is a fan of surfing, hydroponic vertical gardens and good design.
