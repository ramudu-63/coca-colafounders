Friedrich Grosse-Dunker

“Fail forward.”

Friedrich Grosse-Dunker is a Co-Founder of Dark Horse, an agency for innovation development in the area of Design Thinking, Co-Innovation and Co-Creation. He also acts as a coach, speaker, and workshop moderator involved in a number of innovation and collaboration workshops around Germany working with major corporations, the prestigious HPI School of Design Thinking in Potsdam, and a number of startups. He was also the Founder and Managing Partner of CityMob, a company focused on mobile applications and location-based services where he was responsible for product strategy, marketing, and business model generation. 

He has won a number of awards including one for best diploma thesis in the area of business ethics and sustainability. He has lived in Australia, Norway and South Africa, can tell you where to find the best coffee in Berlin, likes football, and loves to travel.
