Efrat Barit

“Seek out for advice, but always follow your instincts.”

Efrat has always been passionate about developing new concepts and bringing ideas to life. After receiving an award during her service at the IDF for successfully managing a war room under extreme conditions, she realized she shines under pressure and thrives in a fast-paced, busy environment.

Over the course of her career, Efrat worked as a product manager in the consumer electronics field. She has been involved in founding several startups and sold her first patent to a leading conglomerate. When she’s not busy hosting one of her famous themed parties, you can find Efrat crafting, designing and even carpentering.
