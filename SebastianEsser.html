Sebastian Esser

“Don’t try to see complexity in problems, break them down into something simple and solve them step by step.”

Sebastian Esser was the Founder and Managing Director of CEO Event, an international network and investment company for leading founders and investors in the digital economy, which was sold to Vertical Media in 2013.

He also supported a number of new ventures and acted as a mentor to several startup companies concerning strategic decisions and online marketing as part of Team Europe Management, which partners with the most talented founders to build sustainable and valuable internet companies. Prior to that, he worked as a senior sales executive for a number of years at Lidl Europe after being poached from SMP Strategy Consultants, a strategy consulting firm which specializes in identifying growth areas and business opportunities. He also founded an independent consulting company for the University of Maastricht and speaks German, English, Spanish, French, and Dutch. Sebastian is an avid handball player and also loves to travel.
